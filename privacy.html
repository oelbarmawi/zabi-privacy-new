Privacy Policy for Zabi

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Introduction

Zabi ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Zabi mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

1. Location Information

  • What we collect: Your device's GPS coordinates when you use the app
  • Why we collect it: To show nearby halal restaurants and provide distance calculations
  • How we use it: Location data is used only during active app usage to display relevant restaurants on the map
  • Data retention: Location data is not stored permanently; it's used in real-time only

2. Restaurant Submission Data

When you contribute a restaurant through our community submission form, we collect:

  • Restaurant name and address
  • GPS coordinates (if you choose to share)
  • Halal certification details
  • Verification source information
  • Optional: Your email address (for follow-up questions only)
  • Optional: Photos you choose to upload

3. Favorite Restaurants

  • What we collect: List of restaurants you mark as favorites
  • Why we collect it: To provide personalized recommendations and quick access
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device and synced to our secure servers via Supabase
  • Data retention: Until you remove favorites or delete your account

4. Device Information

  • What we collect: Device type, iOS version, app version
  • Why we collect it: To provide app support and optimize performance
  • How we use it: For crash reporting and analytics to improve app stability

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Display nearby halal restaurants based on your location
  2. Calculate distances from your current position
  3. Process and verify community-submitted restaurant information
  4. Improve app functionality and user experience
  5. Respond to support requests
  6. Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

Data Storage and Security

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

Supabase (Database & Storage)

  • Purpose: Store restaurant data, user submissions, and favorites
  • Data stored: Restaurant information, user submissions, favorites, photos
  • Security: Industry-standard encryption and secure authentication
  • Location: Servers hosted in secure data centers
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Google Places API

  • Purpose: Restaurant search and autocomplete suggestions
  • Data shared: Search queries, location coordinates
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

  1. Service Providers: With Supabase and Google to provide core app functionality
  2. Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  3. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

We never share your email address or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  1. Access your data: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  2. Delete your data: Request deletion of your submissions and favorites
  3. Opt-out of location: Disable location services in your device settings (this will limit app functionality)
  4. Update information: Edit or delete your restaurant submissions
  5. Withdraw consent: Stop using the app at any time

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: oelbarmawi@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Zabi is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Location Services

iOS Location Permissions

The app requests "When In Use" location permission with the following explanation:

  • "Zabi needs location access to find halal restaurants near you"

You can control location permissions in your device settings:

  1. Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services
  2. Find "Zabi" in the app list
  3. Choose: Never, Ask Next Time, or While Using the App

Note: Disabling location services will prevent the app from showing nearby restaurants and calculating distances.

Camera and Photo Library

Why We Request Access

  • Camera: To take photos of restaurants for community contributions
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos for restaurant submissions

When We Request Permission

Access is only requested when you:

  1. Tap the camera icon in the submission form
  2. Choose to add photos to a restaurant contribution

You can deny these permissions and still use the core app features (viewing and searching restaurants).

Data Retention

  • Location data: Not stored; used in real-time only
  • Restaurant submissions: Retained indefinitely to maintain community database
  • Favorites: Retained until you delete them or request account deletion
  • Photos: Stored until you delete your submission or request removal
